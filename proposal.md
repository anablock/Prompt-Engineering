**pSEO / AEO / SEO Metadata Business Logic (All Websites):**

All four brand websites are managed under a unified search visibility framework covering three distinct layers:

**1. Traditional SEO (Search Engine Optimization)**
- Title tags, meta descriptions, canonical tags, and header hierarchy (H1–H4) maintained and updated monthly across all pages
- XML sitemap generation and submission to Google Search Console
- robots.txt management and crawl budget optimization
- Internal linking strategy and anchor text optimization
- Core Web Vitals monitoring and performance remediation (LCP, CLS, FID)
- Schema markup: LocalBusiness, Dental Clinic, MedicalProcedure, Product, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, Review

**2. pSEO — Programmatic SEO (Scale Content at Volume)**
- Automated generation of location-specific service pages for Streamline Dental (e.g., "Dental Implants in Henderson, NV", "Invisalign in North Las Vegas") using templated page architecture
- Dynamic page generation for The Forever Smile by treatment type × location (e.g., "Full-Mouth Rehabilitation Las Vegas", "All-on-4 Implants Henderson")
- E-commerce pSEO: auto-generated product and category pages for the Forever Smile shop with unique, keyword-rich metadata per SKU
- Programmatic FAQ pages built from patient question data and CRM inquiry patterns
- Automated internal linking between pSEO pages and primary service pages

**3. AEO — Answer Engine Optimization (AI Search: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Bing Copilot)**
- Structured content written to answer specific patient questions in a format AI engines can cite (concise, authoritative, source-attributed)
- FAQ schema and HowTo schema implementation to maximize AI Overview inclusion
- Entity optimization: ensuring Dr. Tran, The Forever Smile, and Streamline Dental are recognized as authoritative entities in AI knowledge graphs
- ChatGPT/Perplexity citation strategy: publishing content on high-authority platforms (Google Business Profile, Healthgrades, Yelp, WebMD partner pages) that AI engines pull from
- Monitoring AI search visibility: tracking brand mentions and citations in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews monthly
- Conversational keyword targeting: optimizing for natural-language queries patients ask AI assistants ("What is the best dental implant option for missing teeth?", "How much does All-on-4 cost in Las Vegas?")
- Structured data for AI consumption: speakable schema, Q&A schema, and MedicalCondition/MedicalProcedure schema across all treatment pages

|---|---|---|---|
